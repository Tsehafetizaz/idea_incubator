About Idea Incubator

The Idea Incubator project was inspired by the need to capture and transform fleeting thoughts into actionable proposals. Whether you're a creative professional or a hobbyist, our tool helps you organize and develop your ideas efficiently. This project is part of our Portfolio Project for Holberton School, showcasing our skills in web development and machine learning.
